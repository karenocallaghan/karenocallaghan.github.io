ICT Service Desk Engineer

Houses of the Oireachtas Service, Kildare House, Kildare Street, Dublin 2

Aug 2010- Jul 2016

Role Description:

  • Helpdesk duties; provide efficient and effective cover when scheduled and prioritise calls to ensure all are logged and cleared.
  • Assets: provision and management of assets for staff and Members; Liaise with Contractor’s to ensure equipment is properly disposed of and service tags are cross checked with records when roll-outs are implemented.
  • Remote Access and USB requests from Member’s and Staff.
  • Constituency Office; Ensuring Members applications are logged and actively liaise with suppliers to ensure broadband is installed in the Constituency offices and contractors keep within the SLA timeframe; Dealing with Members queries regarding, for e.g., ISDN refunds, entitlements.
  • Telecoms; processing invoices in a timely manner as not to incur prompt payments; Liaising with suppliers regarding setting up new accounts, ordering stock and telecoms queries.
  • Electoral Register’s; ensuring receipt & provision to Members with LGCSB timeframes.
